Top 3 most visited nations

France

For more than 30 years, France has been the most visited country in the world with 89.4 million arrivals.
A country renowned for its rich history, diverse culture, museums, the romantic charm of Paris, and stunning landscapes, is home to 49 UNESCO World Heritage sites. Among them, 42 of these are cultural properties, and 6 are natural properties. In addition, the abundantly beautiful countryside is full of fabulous villages, mountains, vineyards, and the occasional castle. Paris, the capital of France, is the most visited city in Europe, with 38 million tourists visiting in 2019. France is anticipated to remain the world's most visited country in 2024, with the influx of tourists driven by the upcoming Paris Olympics.


Cultural Attractions

  • Paris Eiffel Tower
  • Palace of Versailles
  • Alsace Region
  • Mont Saint-Michel

Spain

Spain secured the second spot with an impressive 83.7 million international arrivals. It's no wonder Spain is often regarded as one of the most visited countries in the world. The country consistently tops the World Economic Forum's biannual Travel and Tourism Competitiveness Index, outperforming 140 other nations in 2019, 2017, and 2015.
Its combination of historical richness, sunny coasts, architectural beauty, flamenco music, and bull running experiences keep tourists coming. Besides, the vibrant nightlife of Madrid, the architectural wonders of Barcelona, and the luxurious living experiences of Marbella make it a visitor's hotspot in Europe. Spain also has El Teide, an ancient but not entirely inactive volcano that visitors can walk in the Parque. Nacional del Teide on the Spanish island of Tenerife.


Cultural Attractions

  • Sagrada Familia
  • Alhambra Palace
  • Prado Museum
  • Museu Nacional d'Art de Catalunya

United States

The United States, a country known for its diverse landscapes and iconic cities, consistently ranks among the top travel destinations in the world, securing third place with 79.3 million international visitors.
As one of the most visited countries in the world, it offers an endless list of tourist attractions From the bustling streets of New York to the golden coasts of California and the amusement parks of Florida, there is an endless list of tourist attractions.


Cultural Attractions

  • Statue of Liberty
  • San Francisco Bay
  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • American Museum of Natural History
